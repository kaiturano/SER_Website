We Appreciate You

Building a rover every year is not only an academic challenge, but it can also be very expensive. We are always looking for ways to innovate our designs. How to Sponsor Us

Sponsorships play a pivotal role in enabling our team to develop an optimal rover. If you or your company have an interest in becoming a sponsor, we kindly invite you to review our Comprehensive Sponsorship Packet and select the level at which you wish to make a contribution. All checks should be made out to the Texas Tech Foundation, Inc. Please indicate "Space Exploration Raiders" on the memo line. It is important to note that all donations are eligible for tax deductions, as they are directed towards a nonprofit institution. We look forward to working with you!
