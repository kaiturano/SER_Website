About Us

This is Texas Tech's Student Organization for the NASA Human Exploration Rover Challenge (HERC). The dedicated team consists of skilled aspiring engineers, passionate designers, and space enthusiasts, all collaborating to craft a cutting-edge human-powered rover for a prestigious NASA competition. The project showcases innovative technology, sleek aerodynamics, and robust mechanical systems. With a shared commitment to excellence, this team embodies collaboration, determination, and the drive to push the boundaries of human ingenuity in pursuit of space exploration.
